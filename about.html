about me

Time Travelling is only for The Adventurers

Hi. My name is Nicole. I am an award-winning tour guide who specialises in time travelling to the past. I worked with schools and tourism industries. My role is to guide you through time , allowing you to witness, engage and experience the past.

1989

Ever since I completed the last chapter of my history classes, I wanted to learn more. I started reseaching academic papers and historical archives to know more about particular eras. In 1989, I graduated from Harvard with a Bachelor with Honours in History and Computing. This was the start of my time travelling journey.

2010

Using my knowledge in Quantum Computing, I attempted to simulate and manipulate quantum states that represent different points in space-time. Sophisticated quantum programming languages like Q# were used to access different timelines.The code calculates the exact energy levels, quantum states, and other parameters needed to "lock onto" a specific point in space-time

2017

Leveraging the quantum computer's output, the traveler uses a particle accelerator to generate the necessary conditions for a stable wormhole. This involves manipulating quantum fields to create a micro-wormhole, which can be expanded using exotic matter to a size large enough for human passage.

2025

We officially introduced the concept of Travelling through Time. Being the pioneer of this movement, I was the first ever tour guide for our journey to the past -- Renaissance Era.

2033

As of now, we have time travelled to 9 different eras. The percentage of travellers being lost in time is as low as 5%. With the advancement of technologies, I am currently working with a cybersecurity team to include backup algorithms in the program that can recalibrate quantum states and shut down the wormhole safely.
